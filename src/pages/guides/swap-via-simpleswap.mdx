# Swap via Simpleswap
**Author: BoJack**
    -   Twitter:  [@0xbezzub](https://twitter.com/0xbezzub)

![](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/syG2MPlFq-KIBWPRTktY2EmWsAAU_4HVadmkkME4M-KLb5wpt-knhnd-C6eCOljXDlPmqNCoh_OrC420w7wrn4vWfVEtbEFf48pZBLMLQPO0XnQpMi9b7tOcTfVOT-OUSe6jqrCVrdxCLBWb7z3ulIk)Another way to get $SEI tokens immediately on the Sei network

  
Swap your tokens from other networks directly for [$SEI](https://twitter.com/search?q=%24SEI&src=cashtag_click) in the Sei network.

***

### Step 1

***

Head to [https://simpleswap.io](https://t.co/lZCXTntKXY)

  

1.  Select the token you want to swap.
    
2.  Select Sei from the drop-down list.
    
3.  Enter the amount.
    
4.  Click on "Exchange".
    

![](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/Q-jwHhl-QKW-bQ6aAxRrdt6_0vmfL2zf-0eoxrYCq5GdL2RXu_PvavpADYcaP5X2Ulge-b_RhPIsYuOcxChzK1mIN_pNXtaSeWafZulOfv4tmG7qVeS--X5yl1eWXYqdFwL14QsHEVX4Ig9jeVTj3JU)

***

### Step 2

***

1.  Enter your Sei wallet address.
    
2.  Put a mark! MEMO is not required.
    

> Note! MEMO is required only if you send [$SEI](https://twitter.com/search?q=%24SEI&src=cashtag_click) to CEX

3.  Click on "Create an exchange".
    

![](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/gdqyCq5yTCG6EW76xSSAD-nwBPIWr1GPbAdfODiWsdMDhn-zDyKYRU-5FrJ2xdi5bouFfkXU55FgxB81CMUrI-8FF3rwpiIPakoPBuRAW6mTWnzE_8h-bFFvh09heuwRVgHz1PD4Pzw4Ip8x4finzsY)

***

### Step 3

***

1.  Copy the address and send token you have chosen to swap to this address
    

-   Confirmation can take up to 20 minutes, depending on the network.
    

![](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/ZeR1FLJ2L7Z3BjLpst2QwfcgIyis9ChFx71Rbe8MnBpZuPu1Aut3rSROjOcQ6ZraUdtGypBlurKhQUWo_J-5LIi4qWBb_YeQO27rzbbhP3CmeSoAO1ZyikjDG4cegsrzTvCuQ70QZIf-DMLDoefHhpk)

***

### The end
