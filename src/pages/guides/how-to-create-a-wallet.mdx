In this section, we'll show you how to create a wallet. The best way to use native wallets, like the Compass wallet. Let's get started!

***

## Step 1


1.  Head to [https://compasswallet.io/download](https://t.co/DUHAoSiaOu), and choose the option you prefer.


![](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/BIHoI3MD-DMeBtx6N6LAsN5FWAE3bFwxISE1DbA-CZbPuhuGTmY2fZyg6Zs3HsKMFK5m6o-Xj_KJVXLJGZJkjfKkdIHnopcM4D2pjwgajQ7S-Xz9ttQuCL72zdF01jHxGxRoxOAOJ_tqGAga58wz4ik)
***

## Step 2


After downloading the Compass wallet, you have two options:



1.  Create a new wallet.

2.  Recovery from existing Leap, Keplr wallets or using a recovery phrase, private key.




We will look at creating a new wallet.

![](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/fZ3JJH2xEE7D6f1kybXz5inG6mxDLFG7z5OGUUBtXB3RXD34wFNEDBlHefdjvpr_03n9vsi0UlLR5J-VRkRZu0MuKdtgUNbwMS2mSMBtLR-HK_-EyTAU_DPAjq3q8Z71BoZCFEuPvVyuGwhaz-01hf8)
***

## Step 3



1.  Copy the recovery phrase.




> **IMPORTANT!**

>

> Save the recovery phrase in a safe place.

>

> Never share your recovery phrase with anyone!



2.  Click on "I have saved it somewhere safe".


![](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/ZgfKKRSPVmHI_kWHt4AmbTb6vH5Seq2zaKWGmVUV6cH-ixreWn_PSvSt_amh7NiDIspn6WR88yJnyJxQf44ZXk6F3fbOr-L77GnA7DWLsJCFT_VBltISik-hjBiAW12Rh6A3JUvhiQaqBvVN1x-oGUc)

***

## Step 4



1.  Enter 4, 8, 12 words of saved recovery phrase.

2.  Click on "Confirm".


![](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/wOd2GOpAnXLdteexWMFpqHwDHvv97_ZWOokufqZ0nJN0aKRzIU_xpUqr3Rof7sXAiHHDDlnIkCcdCnfGqr2_cUUv_281Q3wnPVgfDhMPCAHxPQY5ejJyKk_SIQJqMSQVrENIiXCi_EdC2rP12aPG2Lo)

***

## Step 5



1.  Create a strong password.

2.  Click on "Proceed".


![](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/hrh3bN3cW4UgGdK59jLQ4L35wvo35qBnOtDr3vLv6_HOj4-6QG5XkbcPBr8lzVIY8ie5DD7gQopvflAodP3saPtA0sFkJ6NxgWVONSNjHeTkeKNP4R0ukbSp2VHGyq5Esm5P8I1hNk-8LV-M5o3oXUs)
