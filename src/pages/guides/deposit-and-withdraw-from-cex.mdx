# Deposit and withdraw from CEX
**Author: BoJack**

![](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/Bb_CVrKYDv_0-0MF60-8XZHtZRWfVvfKJuzGxT41IKwbLsjI9WJAxtyB-UEpbXjYWz8iWZr1jSLwc6CADnUecCBY_IBVBx8nWoCfae_-NRxVtfRwH1wkbHfUP5F56lbVNsqbTAgt2EiWhWEDZnrYERk)

Let's see how to withdraw and deposit $SEI to a centralized exchange.  
> **IMPORTANT!**

>

> To deposit to CEX from the Sei network - **YOU MUST PROVIDE MEMO!**

>

> To withdraw $SEI tokens from the CEX - memo is not required.

  

-   [Deposit $SEI to CEX]()
    
-   [Withdraw $SEI from CEX]()
    

***

### Deposit

***

#### Step 1

1.  Select Sei from the drop-down list.
    
2.  Copy the address.
    
3.  Copy the MEMO.
    

> **MEMO is always required if you send [$SEI](https://twitter.com/search?q=%24SEI&src=cashtag_click) to CEX.**

![](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/GeAc3jZ0yPUYwBdTHdaxQlYsR5Uau9hSEF5LWZG06WpvPBbC3eRHU6319I1mVP_Ms7DztM08ZqV7V5lQpt-Q2hrrnWX5dMisZzvH95CFNfnfUEd-t4L8Iu4VJn1LDaKzRq6lfGysDTR8xLuaUy60aGc)

***

#### Step 2

1.  Enter the address you copied in the first step
    
2.  Enter the amount.
    
3.  Click on "Review Transfer".
    

![](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/h5BMtx7QP-OtPzQJe5fdWXbqFJ-6LXcFbyavmL47gHfe8C0WOfdFi4B0co5JFYBysAT5eyUZX-v_oj3Pafx9xpjWoJiKnxhaDpNWYSA03SN9DOWbDoUNqYtIAQ4-WPdNUaxbeQPILQADvGV5xtMD0oc)

***

#### Step 3

1.  Paste the MEMO you copied in the first step.
    

> **MEMO is always required if you send [$SEI](https://twitter.com/search?q=%24SEI&src=cashtag_click) to CEX!**

2.  Click on "Send".
    

Congratulations! You've successfully sent [$SEI](https://twitter.com/search?q=%24SEI&src=cashtag_click) to CEX.

### ***  
Withdraw  
***

1.  Select Sei from the drop-down list.
    
2.  Copy & paste Sei address.
    
3.  Enter the amount.
    
4.  Click on "Withdraw".
    

Congratulations! Now you have [$SEI](https://twitter.com/search?q=%24SEI&src=cashtag_click) on the Sei network.
